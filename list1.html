How to Make Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 11/3 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup Nesquick
  • 11/2 cups of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 11/2 teaspoon of vanilla

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, and salt in a small bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat. Stir in Nesquick. Blend in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Blend in dry ingredients. Pour batter into a rectangular pan.
  4. Bake in 350 degress oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
  5. Let cool fully before removing from pan. Enjoy!
